Exchange of Hearts

Exchange of Hearts with Mary

Put, O wondrous Mother, in place of my sinful heart Your Immaculate Heart, that the Holy Spirit may work in me and Your divine Son may grow in me.
Grant my request, O great, O faithful Mediatrix of all graces. Amen.

Exchange of Hearts with Jesus

Put, O good Jesus, in place of my sinful heart Your divine wounded Heart, that the Holy Spirit may work in me and You, Merciful Jesus, may grow in me.
Grant my request, O good, O faithful, and loving Jesus, that You may soon reign as King of Peace over this world. Amen.

Note:

  • The exchange of hearts with Jesus is prepared by the exchange of hearts with Mary. Mary, the great Mediatrix of graces, pours all graces into our hearts and prepares our hearts for the exchange of hearts with Jesus.

  • The exchange of hearts with Jesus transforms our heart and gives grace free rein, that we may recognize and fulfill the will of God. It is the personal total consecration to God with the goal that Christ may reign in us and over the world as King of Peace.

"Not the smallest prayer, not a tear of secret need, not a breath of secret longing directed to God will ever be in vain! Yet in God's own time and in His way they will be blown back in clouds of blessing and fall upon you and upon all for whom you pray in a stream of mercy."
