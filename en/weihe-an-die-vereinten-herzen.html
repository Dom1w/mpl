Consecration to the United Hearts

Consecration to the United Hearts

Eternal Father, You have received the highest glorification from the Most Sacred Hearts of Jesus and Mary. Your divine Son made man, united with His Mother in the spirit of loving reparation, has perfectly fulfilled Your will. We offer You this glorification anew, that You may bless and heal us through these Hearts and send the Holy Spirit to renew the face of the earth.

Divine Redeemer, we acknowledge You as the Son of the Eternal Father, the only Mediator before God. According to the Father's good pleasure, You have united Your holy Mother as Mediatrix and helper in the work of redemption with Your mission. In this spirit of living trust, we desire to consecrate ourselves, our families, and especially the Marian Movement of Priests and Laity to the united Hearts of Jesus and Mary.

We thereby pledge ourselves to live in this spirit of the Hearts of Jesus and Mary and to work that the petitions of the prayer You Yourself taught us may be fulfilled: We desire to strive that everywhere we have influence, Your name may be hallowed, Your kingdom come to us, and all be done according to Your Divine Will. Then You will also bless the earth with daily bread for all. You will forgive us our trespasses and incline our hearts to peace. You will mercifully preserve us from new offenses and finally deliver us from all evil.

This we ask through our beloved Mother and Lady, whom we invoke with confidence with the greeting of the Angel:
"Hail Mary, full of grace, the Lord is with thee. Blessed art thou among women, and blessed is the fruit of thy womb, Jesus. Holy Mary, Mother of God, pray for us sinners, now and at the hour of our death. Amen."
