Marian Movement of Priests and Laity

For the Triumph of the United Hearts of Jesus and Mary e.V.

Our Marian Movement of Priests and Laity for the Triumph of the United Hearts of Jesus and Mary (MPL), founded on December 8, 2021, is oriented in its spiritual direction entirely to the original form of the Militia of the Immaculata founded by St. Maximilian Mary Kolbe. Therefore we have chosen this great Marian apostle and fighter against all heresies, alongside the Mother of God Mary, Mother of the Church, and St. Joseph, Patron of the Universal Church, as special patrons of our new apostolate movement.

The Goals of the MPL

  • Bold witness to the true Catholic faith
  • Veneration of the United Hearts of Jesus and Mary and the promotion of their triumph throughout the world
  • Imitation of the Holy Family of Nazareth through a life in the Divine Will, thereby contributing to the renewal of the Church
  • Mutual strengthening and support on the common path of faith, so as to resist the great temptation to conform to the world and its principles (cf. Rom 12:2; 2 Tim 4:3-5)
  • To give support and orientation to all who are searching for the truth
  • Catechesis
  • Apostolate and mission work

She Who Overcomes All Heresies

She destroys the heresies, not the heretics, for she loves them and desires their conversion. Precisely because she loves them, she frees them from heresy and destroys their false views and convictions.
(St. Maximilian M. Kolbe)

Mary wants to lead every soul to nuptial union with Christ. Yet Lucifer and his minions want to prevent the reign of Jesus in souls. His efforts therefore aim to erase the name of Jesus Christ from the whole earth: He is determined to destroy all who believe in this name. The first target of his attacks is the Catholic Church, for to the Catholic Church God has entrusted all spiritual treasures for all mankind. To realize his plans of destruction, Satan makes special use of Freemasonry. This secret sect founded in 1717 shrinks from no cruelty to utterly eradicate the Catholic Church and every faith in the supernatural world. Yet the Mother of God does not look on idly. With maternal love she wrestles for the salvation of her straying sheep.


In 1917, from May to October, the Immaculate Virgin appeared to three shepherd children at Fatima to offer the last means of salvation: Her Immaculate Heart. Mary called for prayer and sacrifice for the conversion of sinners. She desired the Rosary and consecration to her Immaculate Heart, especially the consecration of Russia, that it might be converted and not spread its communist and leftist ideas in the world. In the same month when the Immaculata gave proof with the miracle of the sun at Fatima that it was indeed she who had appeared there, St. Maximilian M. Kolbe was inspired to found the Militia of the Immaculata. Without knowledge of the events at Fatima, he became the instrument for the plans of the Mother of God. His vision was to consecrate all souls in the world to the Immaculata and through Mary to conquer all hearts for Jesus. What was the reason for his burning zeal? Deeply shaken, on October 13, 1917, as a young student in Rome, he witnessed Masonic processions singing songs in honor of Satan, marching to the Vatican. On banners he read the inscription: „Satan will reign in the Vatican and the Pope will be his servant.“

He writes: When Freemasonry came more and more into the open in Rome and spread their banners before the windows of the Vatican, on which they portrayed the Archangel Michael being trampled and defeated by Lucifer, and distributed pamphlets insulting the Holy Father, the idea arose to found an association to fight Freemasonry and the other servants of Lucifer.

Freemasonry spreads its ideologies through all media, journals and books. All blasphemous tendencies and programs originate predominantly from their workshop. Fr. Kolbe was very well informed: When we look around us, we perceive the alarming disappearance of morality, especially among youth. Associations appear that are truly hellish. (…) They work feverishly, according to the resolutions of the Freemasons: „We will conquer the Catholic Church, not with arguments, but with the perversion of morals!“

In the 19th century the hostile forces penetrated all areas of life. They seeped into Holy Church to spread the ideas and spirit of liberalism in all its forms in seminaries, universities and monasteries. Not just one error, but all heresies that have ever existed were propagated. In „Modernism“ all heresies are packaged together. All errors that have ever attacked and undermined the Church join forces here and advance in a general assault to destroy the Church from within. Ecumenism undermines the Divine Truth that was entrusted exclusively to the Roman Catholic Church by its Founder, Our Lord Jesus Christ.

The fruits cannot be overlooked: The Catholic Church seeks to conform itself to other Christian denominations. The relationship between religions is being redefined as if all religions worshipped the same God and were ways of salvation. For over two centuries the Popes warned against the false ideas. Yet the enemies occupied more and more key positions in politics and also in the Church. After the Second Vatican Council the foreign ideas prevailed in Rome: theological, political, philosophical, democratic ideas utterly foreign to the nature of the Church. They work in her womb like destructive poison. And precisely in opposition to all that has just been described, St. Maximilian M. Kolbe founded the Militia of the Immaculata.

Concrete Means

The following points show us how we can concretely participate in the apostolate for the triumph of the United Hearts of Jesus and Mary:

  • Anchor one's own life entirely in the will of God and foster an intimate relationship with God
  • Regular reception of the holy Sacraments
  • Total consecration to the Immaculata according to the MI Rule
  • Consecration to the United Hearts of Jesus and Mary
  • Vicarious prayer, sacrifice and suffering (we pray especially for the Holy Father, bishops, priests, all consecrated persons and the sanctification of families. The protection of life as well as the conversion of all who err and unbelievers and all enemies of Holy Church should also be a special prayer intention.)
  • Daily Rosary devotion
  • Proclamation and distribution of writings
  • Distribution of the Miraculous Medal
  • Works of charity, donations
