Militia of the Immaculata
 She will crush your head (meaning the serpent). (Gen 3, 15)
You alone have conquered all heresies throughout the world. (Office of Our Lady)

The MI is imbued with the spirit of the "Society of the Mother of God" as Grignion described it. It includes laypeople and those consecrated to God. The rules are so short and simple that they can be lived in every family, every community, every religious order! In a letter to a brother in Rome in 1918,
Fr. Maximilian writes:

Dearest Brother! I am writing this card on the day of the revelation of the Immaculate Virgin Mary of the Miraculous Medal […] Our Most High Immaculate Lady has deigned, after a year of expectation and doubt and uncertainty on our part, to determine the rule for her "Militia" as follows:

I. Goal
 The conversion of sinners, heretics, schismatics, etc., but especially the Freemasons; and the sanctification of all, under the reign and through the mediation of the Immaculata.

II. Conditions

  1. Total consecration to the Immaculata, by giving oneself as an instrument into her hands.

  2. Wear the "Miraculous Medal."

III. Means

  1. Invoke the Immaculata as often as possible every day with this ejaculatory prayer: O Mary, conceived without sin, pray for us who have recourse to thee, and for all who do not have recourse to thee, especially for the Freemasons.

  2. Use all legitimate means as far as possible, in the various states and circumstances of life, in the opportunities that present themselves: this is left to the zeal and prudence of each one; but the special means is the spread of the Miraculous Medal.¹

¹ The rules were written by Fr. Kolbe in the Latin language, the language of the holy Church, so to speak the eternally valid language. (See p. 12)


How do I become a member of the MPL?
 To become a member of the MPL, it is first important to inform yourself about the spirituality of our apostolate movement.
Anyone who feels called and is also willing to contribute concretely to the goals of the MPL is heartily welcome among us.

What else do I need to know?

  • No membership fees

  • No dues

  • No regular newsletters or circulars

  • A Holy Mass is celebrated monthly for the living and deceased members and benefactors of the MPL.

They also receive in a special way a share in the spiritual fruits of the MPL.
